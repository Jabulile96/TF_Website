Services

Sports

We donate sporting equipment to public schools and we run sport competitions for local schools, Ivorypark community and neigbouring communities.

Arts and culture

We donate art and crafts, painting and music instruments. we run talent show competitions for local schools, Ivorypark community and neigbouring communities.

Saftey and Security

We collaborate with registered local patrollers to help keep our cummunity and our schools safe.

Technical school

In a process of registering a techinal school. We are still gathering funds for the stand and the building.

Science fairs

We run spelling B, science fairs and many other science competitions to come.

Recycling

We are combating illegal dumping in Ivory Park and neighnoring communities by running recycling events.

Media and Reporting

We hire photographers and videographers to capture the events we run, that are on our YouTube channel. We plan to use them to help fund the organisation. We have writers who report and help publish newspapers for the organisation.

Counselling and Rehab

We have partnered with Counselling NPOs to help the affected members of the community to deal with trauma, depression, GBV and to fight against addiction.

Tutoring and Tertiary applications

We provide free online Tutoring for grade 10 to grade 12 students, first year tertiary level mathematics and we assist with tertiary applications.

Food and Donations

We donate food, toiletries and other essential needs to active members of the community.

Coaches and Teachers

We help local schools find coaches for sports and art teachers. We would highly appreciate if you volunteer to coach or teach any of our local schools.

Employment and Job Opportunities

We employ and help find job opportunities for active members of the community and volunteers. We also help locals start small businesses.
