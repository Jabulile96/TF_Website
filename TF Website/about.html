About Us

Founded by Miss Jabulile Khoza in August 2022,
with the visionto better deprived societies, the foundation
is on its way to improving the livelihoods of various
communities’ step by step. The founder having lived in
a society that is deprived of the opportunity to better itself
has made it her goal to ensure that her community and others
are provided with more opportunities to develop.
Tiyiselani Foundation aims
to rewrite the narrative of disadvantaged communities.
To rewrite the narrative, our foundation looks at
meeting the needs of financially disadvantaged
schools by organizing various community-based events to
generate a source of income. We intend to give the unemployed
youth in these communities a better future by
providing them with job opportunities that help develop the
society they belong to. We believe that together,
we can be “Stronger with Tiyiselani.”
